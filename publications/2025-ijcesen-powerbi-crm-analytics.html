The Impact of Power BI and Data Analytics in CRM Reporting for Agri-Banking Institutions

Abstract

This study shows how integrating Power BI with CRM systems can improve analytics and operational visibility for agri-lending institutions using star-schema modeling, DAX measures, and interactive dashboards, reporting notable performance and adoption outcomes.
